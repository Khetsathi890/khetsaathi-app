KhetSaathi — Farm Companion App
KhetSaathi ek all-in-one agriculture web app hai jo General audience — किसान (farmers), agriculture students, aur home gardeners — sabke liye design ki gayi hai। App ka goal hai kheti se juda practical gyan aur tools ek hi jagah, simple aur bilingual (Hindi/English) format me pahunchana।
Core Features:
Plant & Crop Library — 14 crops, vegetables, fruits aur home plants ki detailed info (season, water requirement, soil type, common diseases aur unke upay)
AI Disease Detector — Plant/leaf ki photo upload karke turant AI-based diagnosis, symptoms aur treatment suggestions
Calculator Tools — Fertilizer Calculator, Irrigation Water Calculator, aur Yield Estimator, jo crop aur area ke basis pe estimates dete hain
Knowledge Hub — Crop rotation, organic manure, water conservation, soil testing jaise practical topics par short tips
Farmer Community — Q&A section jaha users apne sawal post kar sakte hain aur dusron ke sawal-jawab dekh sakte hain
Bilingual Support — Poori app Hindi aur English dono me switch ho sakti hai
